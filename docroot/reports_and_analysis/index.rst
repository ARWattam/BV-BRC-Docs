Reports and Analysis
=====================

The BV-BRC team produces regular and ad hoc reports of relevance to selected viral and bacterial pathogens for use as references by the BV-BRC user community. If you use information obtained from these reports in publications or presentations, please be sure to cite the BV-BRC website and publication. If you have ideas for other reports that would be helpful to the pathogen research community, please contact us with your suggestions through our HelpDesk.
